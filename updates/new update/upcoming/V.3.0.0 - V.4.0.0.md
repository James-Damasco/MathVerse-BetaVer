## 🔓 How the Unlock System Works

### 🌟 Step 1: Accumulate XP (Experience Points)
XP acts as your permanent skill level. You earn XP by successfully answering math problems, completing quests, and defending realms you have already unlocked. High-streak multipliers grant bonus XP.

### 🪙 Step 2: Mint Coins
Coins are the active currency of the Multiverse. Earn them by:
*   Completing *Daily Math Bounties* (quick-fire mental math challenges).
*   Discovering hidden *Concept Chests* scattered throughout active zones.
*   Participating in weekend community events.

### 🔑 Step 3: Break the Seal
Once your account meets both the Coin and XP thresholds, head to the *Grand Archivarius Hub*, select the locked branch, and spend your resources to instantly generate the new world map, unlocking its respective storylines, items, and mechanics.

## 🛠️ Additional New System Features

### 🔄 The Prestige Re-Lock
Once you 100% master a specific branch of mathematics, you can choose to *Prestige* that realm. This locks the realm again but resets it with elite-tier difficulty, granting you exclusive cosmetic armor (like the Pythagorean Cloak) and double coin yields upon unlocking it a second time.

### 📊 Interactive Lock Dashboard
A clean, visual progression tracker has been added to the main menu. You can easily see a glowing node-map of all math branches, showing exactly how many Coins and XP you need left to break your next seal.
