Concept 3

Splitting Up Big Multipliers

Need to multiply large double-digit numbers quickly in your head? Split the big number up! For example:

12 × 5 is equal to:

(10 × 5) + (2 × 5)

50 + 10 = 60!

Concept 4

The Division Factoring Reversal

To divide any large value easily, ask yourself: *"What multiplied by the divisor gets me the dividend?"* Division is just multiplication backward!

64 ÷ 8 = ?

Flip it: ? × 8 = 64

8 × 8 = 64, so the answer is 8!
